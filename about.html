Welcome to SneakNStreet, India's premier GenZ streetwear supplier!



Our team is made up of passionate fashion enthusiasts with a love for all things modern and streetwear. Our mission is to provide our customers with high-quality, unique and stylish modern streetwear at an affordable price, especially during these times of high inflation.


SneakNStreet was founded in January 2023 in Gujarat, India by Japan Kachhiya , Shivaansh Dave , Probin Bhagchandani. As entrepreneurs at heart, they combined their interests in photography, art, music, and modern clothing to create something truly special.We have searched the world to bring you the best of the best in modern streetwear, carefully selecting pieces from all your favorite brands.


At SneakNStreet, we're more than just a store - we're a community of modern streetwear enthusiasts. We regularly host in-store events and collaborations with local artists and designers, so be sure to check out for the latest updates.


We look forward to helping you find your next favorite piece, whether in-store or online. Join our community of modern streetwear enthusiasts and discover the unique style and aesthetic of SneakNStreet, where the past and the present merge to create something truly special.
